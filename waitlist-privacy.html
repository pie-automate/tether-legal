Tether Waitlist Privacy Notice

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This notice covers how Tether collects, stores, and uses email addresses submitted through the waitlist form on tethermind.app. For information about the Tether iOS app, see our main Privacy Policy.

1. What we collect

When you submit your email address through the waitlist form on our website, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The section of the website where you submitted (hero or final call-to-action) — used for internal analytics only
  • A timestamp of when you submitted
  • A truncated version of your browser's user-agent string — used only for debugging form issues

We do not collect your name, location, IP address, or any other personal information through the waitlist form.

2. Where we store it

Waitlist submissions are stored in a private Google Sheet managed by Tether. Access is restricted to the Tether team. Submissions are also briefly processed by Cloudflare Turnstile for bot protection; Turnstile does not retain your email.

3. How we use it

We will email you one time when the Tether iOS app launches on the App Store. That email will include a link to download the app and a code for a free trial of premium features.

We will not:

  • Sell, rent, or share your email with any third party
  • Send you marketing emails beyond the single launch announcement
  • Add you to a recurring newsletter without explicit opt-in on the launch email

4. How to opt out or delete your email

You can request removal of your email from the waitlist at any time by emailing hello@tethermind.app. Include the email address you submitted, and we'll delete it within 7 days.

Once we send the launch announcement email, it will include an unsubscribe link. Clicking that link removes you from any future communications.

5. Legal basis

We collect your email based on your affirmative consent when you submit the waitlist form. Under GDPR (EU residents) and CCPA (California residents), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tethermind.app.

6. Retention

We retain your email until one of the following occurs:

  • You request deletion
  • The launch announcement has been sent and 90 days have passed without further contact
  • The Tether app launches and we migrate the waitlist to a dedicated email service (e.g., Mailchimp or Beehiiv); in that case, the Google Sheet is archived and deleted

7. Changes to this notice

If this notice is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Material changes will be communicated by email to everyone on the waitlist before taking effect.

8. Contact

Questions about this notice or your data: hello@tethermind.app

This notice supplements the Tether Privacy Policy, which covers the iOS app. Both documents together describe how Tether handles personal information.
